Privacy Policy

SeeTheTime is designed to work without accounts, ads, or personal data collection. This page explains what information is and is not handled in connection with the app.

Last updated: May 2025

Introduction

Sparkling AI AB ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our "SeeTheTime: Visual Timer" mobile application (the "App").

The simple truth: We do not collect, store, or share your personal information.

This privacy policy applies specifically to the SeeTheTime: Visual Timer app and does not cover other applications developed by Sparkling AI AB.

Information We Do Not Collect

SeeTheTime: Visual Timer is designed with privacy in mind. The app:

  • Does not require account creation and does not ask you to sign up or log in.
  • Does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or home addresses.
  • Does not track your activity or monitor how you use the timer.
  • Does not access your photos, contacts, or other apps.
  • Does not contain advertising or ad-tracking systems.
  • Does not include in-app purchases or store payment information inside the app.

Information Collected by Apple

Because SeeTheTime is distributed through the Apple App Store, Apple may collect information related to your purchase, download, and device installation.

  • Purchase transaction details handled entirely by Apple
  • Device information needed for installation
  • Basic usage analytics if you enable them in iOS settings

This information is managed according to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive your payment details from Apple.

Crash Reports

If the app crashes, iOS may generate crash reports that help improve stability. These reports are generated by iOS, contain technical details about the crash, do not include personal information, and are only shared if crash report sharing is enabled on your device.

Notifications

The app uses local notifications to alert you when a timer completes while the app is in the background and to provide timing-related reminders.

  • Notifications are generated locally on your device.
  • No notification data is sent to external servers.
  • You can disable notifications in iOS Settings for SeeTheTime.

Data Storage

All timer settings and preferences are stored locally on your device. This information never leaves your device, is not backed up to our servers, and is removed when you delete the app.

Payment and Billing

SeeTheTime: Visual Timer is a paid app available through the Apple App Store. Apple handles all payment processing.

  • We do not process or store payment information.
  • All transactions follow Apple's payment systems and policies.
  • Refunds and billing questions should be directed to Apple Support.
  • The app does not offer subscriptions or in-app purchases.

Children's Privacy

SeeTheTime is designed for parents, caregivers, and educators. While it may be used in settings involving children, it is not designed to collect any personal information from users of any age.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or social media services. The only third-party interaction is with Apple's systems for distribution and optional crash reporting.

International Users

Because the app does not collect personal information, there are no data transfer concerns related to international use. The app functions locally on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the updated policy on this page, update the "Last updated" date, and if appropriate note the change in the App Store release information.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the app, contact us using the details below.

Email: app-support@sparkling-ai.com

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. To remove app data entirely, simply delete SeeTheTime from your iOS device.
