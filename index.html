AgentPing

Phone-call escalation for agents when a human decision actually matters.

AgentPing lets software agents escalate beyond chat and trigger a real phone call, with retries and acknowledgement flow built in. Designed for developer-facing APIs and urgent operational events where missed notifications have real cost.

  • Voice-first agent-to-human escalation
  • Clear developer-facing API product
  • Strong operational use case for urgent events

AI-powered parking sign reading for everyday drivers.

Reads Swedish parking signs, translates rules into plain language, and helps users avoid fines. Live on iOS and Android, with 3,000+ users reached.
