SeeTheTime

Make time visible and manageable.

A visual timer for parents, caregivers, and educators who want calmer routines, clearer transitions, and a simple way to help children understand time.

  • No account required

Why It Works

Simple enough for routines. Clear enough for shared use.

SeeTheTime keeps the interface focused so adults can set a timer quickly and children can understand it at a glance.

Time made visible

The circular timer gives a concrete sense of how much time is left, which helps make waiting and transitions easier to follow.

Fast to set

The swipe-based interaction is intentionally direct, so the app can support routines without turning into another thing to manage.

Low distraction

The interface stays quiet and focused, making it suitable for home routines, classroom transitions, and one-on-one support.

Built for real use

Responsive layouts support both iPhone and iPad, including portrait and landscape use when the timer is shared in a room.
