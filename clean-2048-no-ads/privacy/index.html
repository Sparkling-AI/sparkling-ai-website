Privacy Policy

Clean 2048 No Ads - Last updated: May 15, 2026

Overview

Clean 2048 No Ads is designed as a simple offline game. The app does not collect personal data, does not show ads, and does not use tracking or analytics SDKs.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal data from the app.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app may save gameplay and settings locally on your device, including:

  • current board state and score;
  • best score;
  • undo state;
  • selected board size;
  • haptic feedback preference.

This local data is used only to make the game work and is not sent to Sparkling AI.

Ads, Tracking, and Analytics

The app does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

Network Use

The game is intended to work offline. If you choose to rate the app or open an App Store link, that action is handled by Apple and is subject to Apple's own policies.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact Sparkling AI at app-support@sparkling-ai.com.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.
